What We Analyse

Powerful Fairness Analysis

A complete fairness toolkit that works end-to-end — from raw data exploration to model comparison to exportable governance reports.

Data Pre-Audit

Before training a single model, understand your data. We analyze representation balance across protected groups, surface proxy variables that silently encode sensitive attributes, and assign a severity rating — Low to Critical.

Fairness Metrics

Six industry-standard fairness metrics computed per protected attribute: Demographic Parity, Equalized Odds, Disparate Impact, and more. Results broken down by group with human-readable explanations.

Model Comparison Engine

Run GridSearchCV across 9 ML algorithms simultaneously. Models are ranked by balanced accuracy minus fairness gaps — so you find models that are both accurate AND fair, not just one or the other.

Proxy Variable Detection

Correlation-based analysis identifies which features act as stand-ins for protected attributes. Priors count, zip code, charge degree — we flag it before your model learns it.

Decision Audit Traces

Every prediction is explainable. Row-level audit trails show which features drove each decision, powered by a local perturbation-based explainer — no SHAP dependency, no cloud calls.

100% Local & Private

Your data never leaves your machine. No external API calls during auditing. All computation runs on your hardware. Optional Gemini integration for plain-English summaries only — and that's opt-in.

The Process

From Data to Decision in 4 Steps

The entire audit workflow runs locally in your browser session. No accounts, no servers, no waiting.

1

Upload Your Data

Drop in any CSV dataset or pick from our included benchmarks: COMPAS Criminal Justice, UCI Adult Income, or German Credit Risk.

2

Configure Attributes

Select your protected attributes (race, gender, age) and your outcome column. Choose to run a pre-audit only, train a new model, or upload an existing one.

3

Run the Audit

Our engine analyzes representation, trains and compares models, computes fairness metrics, and builds row-level decision traces — all locally.

4

Review & Export

Explore interactive results in the dashboard. Download a comprehensive PDF report with full audit documentation ready for compliance review.

Our Mission

Why We Built This

Bias in machine learning isn't hypothetical — it's documented in hiring algorithms, credit scoring, medical diagnosis, and the criminal justice system. Most auditing tools require cloud access, meaning sensitive data leaves your organization.

AI Bias Auditor was built on a single principle: fairness analysis should be accessible, local, and transparent. No SaaS subscription. No data upload to a third-party server. Just a tool you run, on your terms.

Built on top of scikit-learn and Microsoft's Fairlearn library, every metric we compute is grounded in peer-reviewed fairness research.
